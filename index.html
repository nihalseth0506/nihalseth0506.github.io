About Me

Master's student in Mechatronics & Robotics at Hochschule Schmalkalden with hands-on experience in robot learning, vision-guided robotics, real-time robot control, and simulation. I build systems that work — from a reinforcement learning pipeline for Franka FR3 manipulation in MuJoCo (Project FRANK, 90% eval success) to a UR10e visual servoing rig and an energy-optimised 6-DOF UR5 control framework.

My engineering philosophy is grounded in honest, defensible implementation: I'd rather quantify exactly what a system does than overstate what it might do. Whether it's tuning PID loops on a self-built pen plotter or architecting a FastAPI telemetry pipeline, I focus on measurable outcomes and solid fundamentals.

Beyond robotics, I'm drawn to quantum physics and the structure of the universe at the largest scales — the kind of questions that sit at the boundary of what's currently knowable. It's a different mode of thinking from engineering, and I find that contrast valuable.
